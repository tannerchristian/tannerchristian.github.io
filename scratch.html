My Running & Training Priorities

I love optimizing training, recovery, and performance. Here's how I think about improving as an athlete:

  1. Build a strong foundation
    • Consistent weekly mileage
    • Easy runs that actually feel easy
    • Gradual progression (no hero weeks)
  2. Layer in smart intensity
    • Intervals and tempo work
    • Race-specific workouts
    • Planned deload weeks
  3. Lock in recovery & mindset
    • Sleep, nutrition, and hydration
    • Mobility and prehab work
    • Confidence and race strategy
